The Official Quick Challenges List Rules Page

  1. Levels in order to be accepted have to be related to quick in any form (since this is the quick challenges list) such as the level name, dedication to quick, referencing to quick.
  2. Levels cannot have a part that is over 8 inputs per second, this is not supposed to be a spam challenges list.
  3. Levels must be verified legitimately.
  4. Levels can be a layout.
  5. Levels cannot be 2p even if one person verified it.
  6. The level cannot be longer than 30 seconds
