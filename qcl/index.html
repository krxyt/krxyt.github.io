Explanation of what exactly a "quick challenge" is:
A quick challenge is a challenge that is related to quick in any way, can be the name of the level, dedication or maybe something referencing to quick. The level can be a layout, there aren't any specific decoration standards. The challenges cannot exceed 8 inputs per second, we want to prevent the list from becoming a spam challenges list.
